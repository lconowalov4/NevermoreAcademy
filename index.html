Classes We Offer

The academy has many classes to train its students. These classes also included specialty subjects that related to outcasts and not normies.

Botanical sciences

Botanical Sciences is a class that teaches students about different plants and their uses.
Students learn about how the plants adapt, survive and thrive in certain environments

Fencing

Fencing is part of Physical Education. It’s basically a combat sport that involves two opponents using swords.
It takes skill, technique and strategy to master, and it’s a great way to stay in shape.

History of Outcasts

History of Outcasts is a class that covers the history and culture of different outcasts, such as werewolves, vampires, and other supernatural creatures.
It covers how they came to be, how they are perceived by normies, their cultural practices and rituals, and their role in various societies throughout history.

About Us

Founded in 1791, Nevermore Academy is an academic institution that nurtures outcasts, freaks and monsters.
Our mission is to help each student master their extraordinary abilities through world-class academics and unique extracurriculars.
