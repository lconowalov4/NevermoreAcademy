Rave'N ball

The Rave'N ball was an evening of enchantment and wonder. The ballroom was transformed for the occasion, with decorations in the school colors and twinkling lights. The music was upbeat, and students danced the night away, letting loose from the stresses of school life. Many students took the opportunity to show off their fashion sense, with elaborate costumes and makeup on display. The whole atmosphere was one of excitement and adventure, as the students enjoyed the chance to socialize and let their hair down. Overall, the Rave'N ball was a night to remember, with unforgettable music and atmosphere.


Written by student Enid Sinclair.
